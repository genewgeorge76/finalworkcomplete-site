Our Story

Built on Four Generations of Family Craftsmanship

J. Worden & Sons Asphalt Paving was founded in 1984 with a single commitment: deliver paving that holds up for decades, not just seasons. What began as a local Chester, Virginia operation has grown into a multi-state commercial force — without ever sacrificing the family values that started it all.

Note: We are the original J. Worden family business and are entirely independent from any other Worden-named paving operations that may exist in Chester or the surrounding region.

Structural Standard
